Beyond standard tinting, we offer high-tech solutions for specific commercial challenges. From protecting digital privacy to combating vandalism.

Advanced Technologies

  • Data Jammer: A revolutionary architectural film that blacks out digital screens (LED/LCD) to outside viewers while leaving the glass perfectly transparent. Essential for glass-walled meeting rooms handling sensitive data.
  • Anti-Graffiti Film (AGI 100): A clear, sacrificial layer applied to the exterior of glass. If vandalized with paint or scratches, the film can be removed and replaced at a fraction of the cost of new glass.
  • Anti-Fog Film: Prevents condensation and steaming up on mirrors and glass in gyms, bathrooms, and swimming pools.
