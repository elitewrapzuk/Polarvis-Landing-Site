How It Works

Polarvis Data Jammer is a specialized optical film applied directly to existing glass partitions or windows. It utilizes advanced polarization technology to render LED and LCD screens completely black to outside observers.

While the glass remains transparent—maintaining the open feel of your office—sensitive financial data, personal information, and confidential presentations on screens become invisible to anyone walking by. It is the ultimate solution for compliance and visual privacy in modern, glass-walled workspaces.
