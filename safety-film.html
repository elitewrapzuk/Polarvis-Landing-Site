Safety & Security Film
Midlands & UK

Glass is the weakest point of any building. Our high-tensile safety films reinforce existing glass, holding it together upon impact to prevent dangerous shards and deter forced entry.

Protection Levels

  • Safety Film (Clear 4 C): A 4-mil invisible layer that upgrades standard glass to meet health and safety regulations (BS EN 12600). Ideal for preventing injury from accidental breakage.
  • Security Film (Clear 8 C): A heavy-duty 8-mil film designed to withstand significant force. It acts as a powerful deterrent against burglars, slowing down entry attempts.
  • Bomb Blast Protection: Specialized anchoring systems available for high-risk commercial and government buildings.
